Get Portfl on your device

Install Portfl to use it full-screen and offline, just like a native app.

💻 Laptop / Desktop

Best for Chrome or Edge browsers.

  • 1
    Look at the right side of your address bar.
  • 2
    Click the Install icon (it looks like a screen with a down arrow).
  • 3
    Click "Install" in the popup.
📱 Mobile (Android/iOS)

Best for Chrome or Safari.

  • !
    If you see an "Install Portfl" banner at the bottom, just tap the Install button.
  • 1
    On iOS: Tap the Share icon (square with arrow) and select "Add to Home Screen".
  • 2
    On Android: Tap the three dots (⋮) and select "Install app".
